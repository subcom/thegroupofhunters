The Group.

A podcast about darkness, danger, and discovery

The vampires claimed the ones they loved. One night, they dared to fight back.

"The Group" is a gritty actual play podcast that dives deep into the world of supernatural horror, conspiracy, and personal reckoning.

Inspired in part by Hunter: The Reckoning, Delta Green and The World of Darkness, we follow a band of reluctant heroes—citizens and strangers—bound by a shared secret and the will to resist the darkness by any means necessary.

This story blends intense character drama, occult investigation, and visceral action with an adrenaline shot of crime fiction, espionage, and noir with a darker twist.

Recommended for adults.
